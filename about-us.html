About Us

When I was young I always remember having the most fun playing games with my friends. The best moments for me were always when we would have a group of kids all huddled around an old CRT TV trying to beat the next level of which ever game was the flavor of the week. The console that we had the most fun playing was the Nintendo Gamecube. After growing up and apart from my neighborhood friends, I never forgot how much fun we'd have.

When I got older and started to earn enough to have some disposable income I decided to look back on the best moments of my childhood. I decided to start collecting Gamecube games and consoles. That has since grown into having so much stock and too many duplicates that I made the move to open my own online store.
